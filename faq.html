Frequently Asked Questions

What does Slarmoo's Box add?

Slarmoo's Box is a mod of Ultrabox, and has all of the same features that Ultrabox has. However, there are some cool additions to be aware of in Slarmoo's Box:
  • New envelopeable effects: reverb, chorus, bitcrush, frequency crush, panning, distortion, and arpeggio speed
  • Two new envelope types:
    • Pitch: track what your notes do based on the pitch (also called keytracking in other daws). This envelope has an adjustable pitch range setting
    • Fall: Similar to twang, except instead of decreasing quickly to slowly, fall decreases slowly to quickly
  • Individual envelope speeds, bounds, and inversions
  • Increased max envelope count to 16 (from ultrabox's 12)
  • A better spectrum and harmonics editor (zoom, copy and paste)
  • Several new keybinds in the Song player (shorten link, toggle loop, toggle zoom, and copy link)
  • Two new themes: Greyscale and Slarmoo's Box
